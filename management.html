manage function¶

insanities.management.manage(commands)¶

Parses argv and runs neccessary command. Is to be used in manage.py file.

Accept a dict with digest name as keys and instances of CommandDigest objects as values.

The format of command is the following:

./manage.py digest_name:command_name[ arg1[ arg2[...]]][ key1=kwarg1[...]]

where command_name is a part of digest instance method name, args and kwargs are passed to the method. For details, see CommandDigest docs.
